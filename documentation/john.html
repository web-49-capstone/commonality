New User Persona

Persona Info

Name: John Johnson

Age: 24

Role: John just moved to a new town, and he doesn't know anybody here yet

Technology

Devices:

  • Iphone 16
  • Macbook
  • Desktop Computer for everyday tasks

Attitudes and Behaviors

Personality: John is friendly and sociable once he gets comfortable. He’s introverted in unfamiliar environments, which makes it hard for him to take the first step in meeting people.

Behaviors: He checks his phone frequently and is comfortable using technology. He's downloaded meetup apps in the past but found them used for larger meetups or for dating.

Frustrations

  • Feels isolated when arriving in a new city and doesn’t know where to start
  • Existing social apps focus too much on dating or don’t lead to real meetups
  • Hates filling out long sign-up forms or answering endless questions

Goals

  • Be able to sign up for a new app effortlessly with a clean, simple UI
  • Find and connect with people nearby who share his interests
  • Create a profile without filling out a ton of unnecessary info

User Story

As a new user, I want to be able to sign up fast and effortlessly to start matching with people.

Use Case

Scenario: John is new to town and wants to meet people who share his interests. He finds the website online and decides to look into it.

Preconditions: John has just landed on the website and is prompted to create an account.

Postcondition: John completes the sign-up process, sets up his profile, selects his interests and is ready to start receiving matches.

Interaction Flow

  1. John opens the website for the first time.
  2. App shows him a brief welcome screen explaining what the app does.
  3. John taps “Sign Up” and chooses to sign up with email or social login.
  4. App then takes him to a profile creation page.
  5. John enters his name, age, and location.
  6. App then prompts him to add his top interests tags to his profile along with a bio and profile photo.
  7. John uploads a profile photo and writes a short bio about what kind of activities he's looking to do with others and his interests tags.
  8. He taps “Finish,” and the app confirms that his profile is complete.
  9. App then takes him to the home page that now shows him potential local matches with shared interests.
