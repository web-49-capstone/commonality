User Story / Use Case / Interaction Flow 2

User already has an account, is looking for new matches, finds 1-on-1 and group options

Name: Sammy Servesg√ºt

Profession & Bio: Sammy is a working professional in an office setting. They push paper and press buttons all day fixing decimal errors in a corporate database. Sammy also loves to play tennis, but has trouble finding a tennis partner. Sammy's significant other, workmates, and old friends are not interested in starting to play tennis. Sammy doesn't want to teach someone new anyways, but wants to find a reliable partner to play with on weekends.

Technology:

Devices:

  • Windows 10 Desktop and Windows 11 Laptop using Chrome
  • Samsung Galaxy Phone using Chrome Mobile
  • Sammy has unlimited access to wifi and data

Love/Hate:

  • Sammy is very good with technology, has competed in MS Excel charity tournaments
  • Sammy knows their way around technology but does not love having to work ON it after work hours

Proficiency:

  • Sammy is able to sort out almost any IT problem but prefers to call the IT department, just in case
  • If an app or program doesn't seem to work, Sammy will sooner delete the app than try to fix it

Attitudes and Behaviors:

  • Sammy is a busy person. Sammy wants to search for tennis partners while running from one meeting to another, on their lunch break at work, or for a few minutes before making dinner each night.
  • Sammy expects a lot of their tech, and will drop an app quickly if they become frustrated by the UX or find the program unhelpful.

Frustrations and Needs:

  • Part of their frustration in finding a tennis partner is the time and effort it takes to meet people and then find out if they also like tennis.
  • Sammy wants to get straight to the point. "I'm not here to hang, talk about work, or have coffee. I don't have much time and I want to play some damn tennis"
  • Sammy needs a way to find someone to do this activity with while cutting through the rest of the fluff that comes with meeting new people.

Goals:

  • Sammy has one goal: to meet a tennis partner, agree on a time, get to the courts, and play a few matches.
  • They would be willing to meet up during lunch breaks, after work, or on weekends.

User Story:

  • As a user of the app, I will try weekly to match with other people who want to play tennis in my area.
  • As a user of the app, I will check my messages daily to chat with matches about skill level and availability.
  • When I feel I've met a good match, I'll use the chat function to schedule a time and place to play with my partner.

Use Case

Sammy and their colleague Beth were chatting at the water cooler about interests, and Beth mentioned this great new website. Sammy set up an account last week, and today is opening the app to use the matching function.

  • Pre Condition: Sammy already has an account set up and interests added, and is on lunch break ready to find matches.
  • Post Condition: Sammy has requested to connect with two potential matches and is waiting on a response.

Interaction Flow:

  • Sammy opens the website on their browser at work
    • The website prompts Sammy to log in with their username/password
  • Sammy enters their username and password and clicks "login"
    • The website takes Sammy to their Home Page, where they can see their profile, existing connections, and can start a new matching session
  • Sammy already has tennis listed as an interest in their profile. They click on "find a new match"
    • The website prompts Sammy with a list of checkboxes that match the interests in Sammy's profile
  • Sammy clicks the box for "tennis" and clicks "find a match"
    • The website presents Sammy with another profile that is interested in tennis
  • Sammy doesn't like the first one, and clicks "next"
    • The website presents Sammy with the next random pairing, this time a group of 3 looking for 1 more
  • Sammy clicks "connect with this group"
    • The website says "request to connect sent! Keep searching?"
  • Sammy clicks on "keep searching"
    • The website shows Sammy another individual's profile
  • Sammy likes this profile and clicks "connect with this person"
    • The website again says "request to connect sent! Keep searching?"
  • Sammy realizes they're out of time, and closes the app. Sammy will wait for notifications.
